Our FAQ

Here are some of our most frequently asked questions

Q: If I decide to list my home charger on EVE, how do I make money?

A: You decide how much you want to charge for the use of your charger. EVE will inform you of the average price charged by other home charger owners in your area, and you will receive periodical notifications about any changes in the average price in your area.



Q: How do I know that I can trust the person who is coming to my driveway/garage?

A: We’ve designed a set of functionalities to ensure trust between the different users of the app. Your address will not be displayed unless someone books your charger. Once you receive a booking, you will also get info about the driver (name, the type of car they drive, the rating they received from other home charger owners). Should you wish to cancel the booking based on this information, you will be free to do so. At the end of the session, you will be asked to rate the user and provide feedback on your session. Users who reach an average rating of less than 4,6 out 5 will not be able to use the app anymore.



Q: Who is liable if any damages are done to my property?

A: It is the driver’s responsibility to leave your property in the same condition in which he or she has found it.

Q: How can I make sure that my charger will still be available to me when I need it?

A: You can specify the days of the week and the time of day when you would like your charger to be made available. At the same time, once someone books your charger, it will not be displayed on the map until it becomes available again. In the unlikely situation that a driver leaves their car in your driveway, you should reach our customer service department so we can contact the driver or escalate the situation with authorities.
